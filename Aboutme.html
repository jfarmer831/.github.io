LEADERSHIP MINDSET

  • Leading should be a learning experience for all members, those who are guiding and those who are guided.
  • Making a connection with everyone on your team is essential to the overall success of a project.
  • Setting clear expectations in the begining will save time and resources.

PHILOSOPHY ON EDUCATION

I believe that education should be:

  • Easy to access.
  • Taught using the lastest cutting edge technology.
  • Taught by those who truly have a passion to relay information to others with the intent to make their lives better and not for the simple sake of profit.

COLLABORATION/TEAMWORK

  • All for all, everyone plays a part, no matter what the contribution percentage is.
  • When we make use of everyone's talent the project will get done efficiently and effectively.
  • There is always something to learn from every member on your team.
  • Having diversity of thought in problem solving is essential to the growth and success of an organization.
